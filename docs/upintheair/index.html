Up in the Air Quintet

Take the Great American Songbook and combine it with some original pieces and you get the Up in the Air Quintet. We are inspired by the tradition of Miles Davis, John Coltrane, Horace Silver, Frank Sinatra and Antonio Carlos Jobim. Crisp flowing articulation and a distinctive swing style have elevated this jazz quintet to another level.
